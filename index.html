Hi, I'm Bo Zhou! Welcome to my personal website!

I joined the Department of Industrial and Operations Engineering at the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in September 2022, working with Prof. Siqian Shen and Prof. Ruiwei Jiang. I completed my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at Huazhong University and Science and Technology in June 2022, where I was supervised by Prof. Jinyu Wen at the Smart Grid Operation & Control (SGO) Lab.

My research interests focus on the mathematical optimization and learning techniques for flexibility and resilience of power/energy systems under uncertainty, which is critical in promoting the sustainable and low-carbon transition of the energy sector. Toward this target, I think theory and applications of energy storage will be the most critical enabling technologies.
