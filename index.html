Hi, I'm Bo Zhou! Welcome to my personal website!

I joined the Department of Industrial and Operations Engineering at the University of Michigan at Ann Arbor as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in September 2022, mentored by Prof. Siqian Shen. I completed my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering at Huazhong University and Science and Technology in June 2022, where I was supervised by Prof. Jinyu Wen at the Smart Grid Operation & Control (SGO) Lab.

My research interests focus on the flexibility and resilience of power/energy systems under uncertainty, which is critical in promoting the sustainable and low-carbon transition of the energy sector. Toward this target, robust optimization and continuous-time optimization are the core techniques in my study and I think energy storage will be the most critical enabling technologies.
